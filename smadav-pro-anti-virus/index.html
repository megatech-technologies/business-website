What Is Antivirus Software?

Antivirus software is a security program designed to detect, block, remove, and prevent malicious software from infecting your computer. These malicious programs include viruses, trojans, ransomware, spyware, worms, and hidden background processes that silently damage your system or steal information.

Modern malware operates quietly. You may not even notice anything wrong until your computer becomes slow, files disappear, accounts are hacked, or sensitive data is leaked.

An antivirus works like a digital guard. It scans files in real time, monitors system behavior, blocks suspicious actions, and alerts you before damage happens.


Why Antivirus Protection Is Important

Many users think they are safe because they browse carefully. Unfortunately, viruses now spread through:

  • USB flash drives

  • Email attachments

Without antivirus protection, you risk:

  • Loss of important files

Installing antivirus is not about fear — it is about responsibility. It protects your productivity, your privacy, and your peace of mind.
